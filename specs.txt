

Console Commands

	resign	- concede the match
	quit	- quit the app
	restart
	
MenuCommand

Console App
	Simple console app to play chess against another player on network.
	
	usage: app.exe game_id [-create|-join] # creator is always white, joiner black
		Creator:
			Broadcast game and wait for a joiner.
		
		Joiner:
			Search for game_id and join it.
